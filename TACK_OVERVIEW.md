Tack One / TackEvo - Company & Product Reference

Saved: 2026-02-25
Purpose: Quick internal reference for supporting and creating value for Tack (Tack One Pte Ltd / TackEvo).

Company overview
- Legal / brand: Tack One Pte Ltd (consumer brand: Tack). Enterprise arm: TackEvo.
- Focus: Location devices (consumer GPS trackers) and location intelligence (enterprise IoT & data services).

Main products
1) Tack GPS
   - Multipurpose GPS tracker for people, pets, and assets.
   - Hybrid tracking: GPS + Wi‑Fi positioning + cellular.
   - Battery: rechargeable, up to ~30 days (standard mode) on 750 mAh battery.
   - Coverage: selected countries (unlimited data roaming in 30+ countries for some SKUs).
   - Features: location history (up to 7 days), safe zones (up to 4), one‑click SOS, meeting point, location masking.
   - No Indoor Elevation Finder™ or fall detection (these are Plus features).
   - Subscription: affordable monthly fee (example cited US$2.95/month) and 30‑day free trial.

2) Tack GPS Plus
   - Adds Indoor Elevation Finder™ (indoor vertical positioning), fall detection, and global roaming (120+ countries).
   - Same long battery life, hybrid tracking and safety features.
   - Target: travelers, enterprise deployments, users needing indoor vertical location.

Software / Platform
- OurSphere app (iOS & Android): user-facing app to pair devices, view live location, history, manage zones, SOS, sharing and privacy controls.
- Support portal: support.tackgps.app with knowledge base, FAQs, manuals, ticketing.

Enterprise (TackEvo)
- Focus: location intelligence, data pipelines, analytics for smarter cities, logistics, environmental monitoring, emergency response and AI models that use location data.
- Offers APIs and platform services for enterprises to ingest and act on location data.

Support & Service
- 1 Year warranty with 1-to-1 exchanges for faulty devices.
- 30‑day free subscription included with purchase.
- 14‑day return policy.
- Privacy & cybersecurity: CSA approval mentioned; privacy policy available on support site.

Key differentiators (claimed)
- Indoor Elevation Finder™ (unique indoor vertical positioning in multi-storey buildings) on Plus model.
- Hybrid location (GPS + Wi‑Fi + cellular) improves indoor accuracy compared to Bluetooth-only trackers.
- Long battery life (~30 days) versus many trackers that last days.
- Global coverage (Plus model) and low subscription fees.
