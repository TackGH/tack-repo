TACK GPS - Extracted Specs (source: https://www.tackgps.app/pages/features and product pages)

Physical:
- Size: 47 x 37 x 17 mm (1.8 x 1.4 x 0.65 in)
- Weight: 30 g / 1 oz
- Battery: 750 mAh rechargeable battery; up to ~30 days on standard mode
- Sensors: Tri-axis accelerometer
- In the box: Tack GPS Device (embedded SIM), USB-C charging cable, wrist lanyard

Connectivity & Coverage:
- Hybrid Location: GPS, Wi-Fi Positioning System (WPS), Cell Tower Triangulation, Bluetooth
- Network: NB-IoT / Cat M1 / GPRS (SIM included)
- Supported countries/providers: USA (AT&T, T-Mobile), UK (O2), Australia (Telstra), Austria (Magenta), Belgium (Orange), Canada (Telus, Bell), Denmark (Telenor, Telia), Estonia (Elisa), Finland (DNA, TeliaSonera), France (Orange), Germany (Deutsche Telekom, Vodafone, O2), Hungary (Vodafone), Italy (Vodafone), Israel (Pelephone), Iceland (Siminn), Japan (Softbank, NTT Docomo, KDDI), Latvia (Latvian Mobile), Luxembourg (Orange, P&T), Mexico (AT&T Mexico, TelCel), Netherlands (Vodafone Libertel, T-Mobile), New Zealand (Vodafone, Spark), Norway (Telenor, TeliaSonera), Poland (Orange Polska), Romania (Orange Romania), Slovak Republic (Orange), Slovenia (SiMobil), Singapore (Singtel), South Korea (KT), Spain (Orange, Telefónica), Sweden (Telia, Telenor), Switzerland (Swisscom), Taiwan (Chunghwa).

Subscription (Tack Cloud):
- SIM card and cellular data included with plans
- Standard vs Premium feature differences: location history 14 vs 90 days; reporting intervals and emergency intervals differ
- Pricing (per device):
  - Quarterly: US$4.95 (Standard) / US$6.95 (Premium) per month
  - Yearly: US$3.75 / US$5.75 per month
  - 2-year: US$2.95 / US$4.95 per month
- Notes: 30 Day free trial (first month)

Warranty & Returns:
- 14 Day Return Policy
- 1 Year Warranty (1-to-1 exchange for faulty devices)
- Certifications: CE & FCC (features page also mentions CSA cybersecurity approved)

Notes & Gaps:
- Product pages reiterate warranty/returns but have limited technical detail beyond features page.
- No separate explicit list of supported countries for the "Plus Global" variant was found beyond the features/coverage list above; confirm if Plus adds more countries (site marketing claims 120+ for Plus vs 30+ for base device).
