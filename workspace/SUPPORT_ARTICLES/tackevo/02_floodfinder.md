FloodFinder™ — Building Flood Resilience in Urban Environment (in collaboration with Infineon Technologies)

Why Broad Forecasts Aren’t Enough—The Need for Hyper-Local, Real-Time Flood Data

The Challenge: Existing Flood Detection Solutions are Under-Deployed, Complicated & Expensive.

The Future's Palm-Sized Solution is here!

TackEVO FloodFinder™ detects and reports floods in real-time, enabling faster response and damage prevention while gathering granular, street-level data to enhance urban planning with AI.

Models & Application:
- B200: barometer-based, up to 2m — for sealed-pipe setups (traffic pillars, bollards, poles)
- U300 / U1500: ultrasonic, up to 3 / 15m — open water surfaces (small bridges, canal walls)
- R5000: radar, up to 50m (coming soon)

Features: solar-powered, weatherproof, built-in battery, 4G IoT/GSM connectivity, GPS geo-tagging, push/email/SMS alerts, AI-driven processing.
